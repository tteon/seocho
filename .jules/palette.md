* The Palantir AIP theme colors used in the `evaluation/static/styles.css` file (`--accent-green` `#3fb950` and `--accent-blue` `#2f81f7`) do not provide sufficient contrast when paired with a white foreground (`color: #fff`). To meet WCAG 2 AA contrast guidelines, button text using these backgrounds must use a darker text color (e.g., `#0d1117` or `#000000`) instead of `#ffffff`.
